## What this is

A running collection of essays, notes, and working-out-loud posts on the topics I'm studying and thinking about as I build toward a PM career.

Writing forces me to make my thinking explicit. If I can't explain a concept clearly in prose, I don't actually understand it yet.

## What I write about

- Product management frameworks and how to apply them without the jargon
- Industrial engineering ideas that map cleanly to product work (process flows, bottleneck analysis, variation reduction)
- The practical reality of transitioning into PM from a technical background in a non-Western market
- Data analysis approaches that inform better product decisions

## Why public

Keeping notes private is low-stakes. Publishing them means I have to be right, or at least clearly wrong in a way I can learn from. The accountability is the point.
